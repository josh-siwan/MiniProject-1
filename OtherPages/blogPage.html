Daily Summary...
Although massive efforts are underway to ensure that hospitals
aren’t overwhelmed with COVID-19 patients—such as a
hospital ship opening in New York and automakers making ventilators—it
still may not be enough, according to Ashish Jha.
“I still don’t think we’re doing [things] as seriously as we need to,”
he said. If people think that “this is somehow just
going to affect the big cities on the coast, I think a lot of places
are going to get into a lot of trouble. And they may not
have the resources that New York has to throw at this problem, and that’s
what I worry about,” he said. But one silver lining is
the fact that the disease will peak at different times in different places.
“Because everybody is getting hit at a different time, we
can actually expand the set of resources we have by sharing
more effectively,” he said.
